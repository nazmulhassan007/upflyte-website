Upflyte Features

Upflyte is designed exclusively for pilots by pilots. Explore the comprehensive suite of features that streamline flight planning, scheduling, document management, and integrated logbooks.

Application Features

Upflyte provides a wide variety of features that are best classified into four main categories: Dashboards, Interactive Calendars, Flight Scheduling, and Integrated Logbooks.

Dashboards

The Dashboard provides important information in one place. Upcoming flights, expiring medicals and currencies, registrations, airport information, weather, logbooks, and even company notifications.

Interactive Calendars

Our Interactive Calandar provides data for all your needs. Default aircraft and Aircrew but with custom categories to enhance the user experience. Schedule Training, Renter, and Commercial Flights, or Maintenance.

Flight Scheduling

Quickly prepare flight schedules and easily complete forms for tracking the date, time, aircraft, passengers, instructors, airports, notes about the flight and documents, to fly more, fly often, and be a better pilot.
